Right Form of Verbs

1. Always, regularly, daily, often, sometimes, normally, generally, everyday, occasionally
ইত্যাদি থাকলে present Indefinite Tense হয়।

* Zahid often (take) tea.
* Zahid often takes tea.

2. Before/after থাকলে একটা verb past indefinite হয় অন্যটা past perfect হয়।

* He came after I (eat) rice.
* He came after I had eaten rice.

3. Already, recently, just, just now, lately, yet, ever, immediately, in the mean time থাকলে verb Present Perfect tense হয়।

* Muni not (choose) her career yet.
* Muni has not chosen her career yet.

4. Once, last, yesterday, ago, one day, in the past, long since থাকলে verb past Indefinite tense হয়।

* He (return) last night.
* He returned last night.

5. (a) Am, is, are, was, were, be, being, been, having + verb (be verb থাকলে passive voice হয়। verb এর p.p হয়। যেমন:

* Rice is (sell) → sold.
* The festival (hold) → The festival was held.

(b) Hold, locate, bear, situate + verb থাকলে passive voice হয়।

(c) Now, at this moment, day by day, ইত্যাদি থাকলে Present Continuous tense হয়। যেমন:

* She is (write).

6. Can, could, may, might, shall, should, will, would, need, dare, must, had better, would rather, would better ইত্যাদি থাকলে verb এর base form হয়।

* Imran can (play) football.
* Imran can play football.

7. (a) To এর পর verb এর base form হয়। যেমন:

* He wanted to (live) Dhaka.
* He wanted to live in Dhaka.

(b) Be used to, with a view to, look forward to, get used to, with an eye to, be accustomed to, be opposed to, mind, worth, cannot help, could not help, confess ইত্যাদি preposition (in, on, over, upon, into, of, by, with, without, before, after, away) থাকলে verb এর সাথে ing হয়।

* Munira is devoted to (read) Quran. → reading.

বেশ কিছু verb এর সাথে to থাকলে ও verb এর সাথে V1 হয়:

* Alam is used to (drive) a car. → driving.
* Imran used to (drive) a car. → drive.

8. (a) Stop, finish, miss, risk, answer, continue, admit, deny, avoid, enjoy, fancy, imagine, delay, suggest, postpone, involve, practice ইত্যাদি verb এর পর gerund form (verb এর সাথে ing) হয়।

* You should try to finish (write).
* You should try to finish writing.

* Zahid tried (do) the work.
* Zahid tried to do the work.

9. (a) Fancy, wish, it is time, it is high time ইত্যাদি থাকলে verb এর past tense হয়। যেমন:

* Ruhul wishes he (sing) a song.
* Ruhul wishes he sang a song.

(b) Fancy, wish, it is time, it is high time, as if, as though, if থাকলে be verb এর past subjunctive were হয়।

* Ruhul wishes he (be) a king.
* Ruhul wishes he were a king.

10. As if, as though, since থাকলে verb এর সঙ্গে Present Ind., Past Ind., Past Perfect tense হয়। যেমন:

* Shohel talks as though he (become) a leader.
* Shohel talks as though he became a leader.
* Shohel talks as though he were a leader.

11. (a) If Present Ind.
* If Tarek comes, I (go) to his house.
* If Tarek comes, I will go to his house.

(b) If `viiv Past Ind.
* If he came, I (go) there.
* If he came, I would go there.

(c) If Past Perfect tense
* If Muni had told me this, I (buy) a car.
* If Muni had told me this, I would have bought a car.

(d) Had + Past Perfect tense থাকলে would have/ could have/ might have এর সাথে verb এর p.p হয়। যেমন:

* Had Muni told me this, I (buy) a car.
* Had Muni told me this, I would have bought a car.

12. V + object + verb এর সাথে ing থাকে যেমন:

* I saw him (play) card.
* I saw him playing card.

13. Go + verb এর সাথে verb এর base form থাকে। যেমন:

* (Go) home, Ruhul will sleep.

14. Objective এর possessive case এর পর verb এর সাথে ing হয়। যেমন:

* Zahid insisted on my (go) his home.
* Zahid insisted on my going his home.

15. Since/for এর পর Present Perfect Continuous tense হয়। যেমন:

* Ruhul (read) in Tilokpur College for two years.
* Ruhul has been reading in Tilokpur College for two years.

Since/for এর পরে sub verb এর পরে have/has থাকে।

16. Next, tomorrow, ইত্যাদি phrase থাকলে Future Indefinite হয়। Subject এর পরে will থাকে।

* He (return) home next day.
* Will return.

17. While এর পরে (sub + V) Past Continuous Tense হয়। যেমন:

* While (Walk) I saw him.
* Walking.
* While he (Walk) I saw him.
* Was walking.

19. Clause এর আগে lest থাকলে, verb এর পরে subject থাকে না এবং should থাকে। যেমন:

* He walked fast lest he (miss) the train.
* Might miss.

Subject-verb Agreement

20. Be এর পরে am, is, are, was, were ইত্যাদি থাকে। Present Tense হলে am, is, are এবং Past Tense হলে was, were হয়। যেমন:

* The poor (be) happy.
* The poor are happy.

21. Sub এর পরে verb singular হলে is হয়। যেমন:

* Ten kegs (be) heavy weight.
* Is.

22. Wh এর পরে subject থাকলে verb হয়। যেমন:

* It is I who (be) to blame.
* It is I who am to blame.

23. Nobody, no one, none, somebody, someone, something, each, every, either of, neither of, any, one, each of, every এর পর subject singular হয় এবং verb singular হয়। যেমন:

* Something (be) wrong.
* Something is wrong.
