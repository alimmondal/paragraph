1. Environment polution/
Climate Change

The main elements of environment air, water and land. This elements should be free from pollution for healthy living. But when the balance of these elements hampered, it is called environment pollution. Air is polluted by smoke and gases from mill and factories. Then water and soil is polluted from industrial waste and from chemical fertilizer, insecticides used by farmer. And then odor pollution is caused by human waste. These pollutions are a great threat to our existence. They cause various human diseases. It changes world’s climate and causes natural disasters. Thus the world is getting unsuitable for living. So, to make life healthy and comfortable, we should keep the environment clean and pollution free.
